

ctrl + b: hide or open side bar
ctrl + `~: hide or open terminal
ctrl + p: search files
ctrl + up or down arrow: scroll with keyboard
ctrl + \: open split window
ctrl + w: close window
ctrl + enter: go to next line even if we're in the middle of a line
shift + alt + down arrow: copy above line
ctrl + backspace: delete a word
ctrl + delete: to delete word ahead of cursor
